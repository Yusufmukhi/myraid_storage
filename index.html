FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about mobile compactor storage systems and our solutions.

What is a mobile compactor storage system? +
A mobile compactor is a high-density storage solution using movable racks on rails — eliminating fixed aisles and saving up to 70% floor space. Used in offices, warehouses, banks, and hospitals across India.
How much space can a compactor save? +
Our mobile compactor systems can save 60–70% of floor space by removing unnecessary fixed aisles, allowing you to store significantly more in the same footprint.
What industries use mobile compactors? +
Banks, hospitals, government offices, warehouses, factories, libraries, defence establishments, railways and educational institutions all benefit from mobile compactor systems.
What warranty do you provide? +
We provide a 1-year warranty against material defects on all storage systems. Terms may vary based on product type and project scale.
What is the price of a mobile compactor? +
Price depends on size, material specifications and customization. We provide detailed customized quotes after understanding your space and needs — at no charge.
Do you offer pan-India delivery and installation? +
Yes. Myriad provides full PAN India supply, delivery and professional installation — from initial site survey to final handover.
Are systems customizable in size and color? +
Absolutely. All our storage systems can be fully customized in dimensions, load capacity and powder-coat color to match your requirements.
How long does installation take? +
Installation time depends on project size and complexity. Most standard installations complete within a few days. Our team provides a timeline estimate at the quotation stage.
